Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

500g chicken
2 cups Basmati Rice
2 onion (sliced)
2 Tomatoes (chopped)
1 cup Yogurt
Gingrt garlic paste
Biryani Masala
Salt
Cooking oil

Preparation steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook thoroughly.
  6. Boil rice until 70% cooked.
  7. Layer rice and chicken mixture in a pot.
  8. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy.

Cooking Information
